Rule Engine

In just a few clicks from an available set of rules, you can choose those rules that are more suitable for your business, for example: If your buyer is trying to pay for 10 units of goods then we send this payment for an additional check, this status is called a "Manual Check", but if the buyer pays for less than 10 units of goods then the transaction is processed automatically, it receives the status "Complete". If the rule you want was not listed in the list let us know and we will add it for you.

Risk Score

Based on the analysis of your visitors, we give them a rating of 0.1% to 100%. 100% will mean a high risk, 0.1% is a very low risk. You can independently choose the most comfortable for you percentage and assign one of the three actions to the selected value which allow either to process the order automatically or send it for an additional check, or reject the order. You can manage these actions with the help of a convenient slider.

Country Rules and Black List

With one click you decide which countries you accept and which ones are rejected, or whether you send them for manual checking. This tool is a list of all countries with the ability to add one of the satatuses: "Approve" "Manual Chack" and "Decline" to each country. Also, based on the statistics of previous years, we have calculated high-risk countries and set the status as manual check by default. At any time, you can change it to your option.

SMS Gateway and 2 factor Authentication

With this tool, you can implement features such as two-factor verification of the user or an additional check of your buyer directly at the time of purchase. You can use Google Code Generator or SMS Gateway as you wish, either together or separately. So you can reliably protect your users from hacking the account, and from stealing your personal data. What Is Fraud Protection?

Fraud Protection is specialized software and hardware complexes that provide monitoring, detection and control of the level of fraud. Systems are oriented primarily for banks, telecom operators, payment systems. With the help of ecommerce fraud protection is possibility of keeping the business safe from the Internet scams. Nowadays using fraud protection services become more crucial and demanded among Internet users.

Relevance of the problem

As it is shown by numerous studies, the volumes and incomes of e-commerce are constantly growing on a global scale. It is predicted that the greatest breakthrough will be shown in such locations as Europe and Asia. In its turn the level of fraud is growing in the same progression or even faster. The number of fraud cases increased by 19% compared to 2013, and it is the fourth consecutive year, when fraud growth exceeds the growth of e-commerce. Out of every $100 in turnover, scammers are currently stealing 5.7 cents. However, fraud is not only related to payments on credit cards. Scammers are becoming more sophisticated in using different kinds of software to manage online banking accounts through gadgets, phones and computers.

Types of Fraud

There are different types of fraud like phishing, pharming and so on. However let’s concentrate more on that type that have deal with business not only on data theft. The bright examples are friendly fraud, affiliate fraud and merchant fraud. Friendly fraud sounds more friendly than it is actually is. It means that customer order the good or the service and pay for it in most cases by credit card. However, then he or she initiates a refund, claiming that sum of money from credit card was stolen. In most cases money was reimbursed even if the customer has already gotten the purchased good or provided service. This method of fraud is especially common with services such as gambling (casino, etc.). Friendly fraud is also carried out in combination with re-shipping. The next one is affiliate fraud. It could be fulfilled by real person who uses fictitious accounts or could be fully automated process. This type of fraud is neutral in terms of risks for payment methods, but it is extremely widespread. Merchant fraud is another method of fraud, which should be also mentioned. The scheme of it is really famous and simple. The goods are offered at low and luring prices, but they are never delivered. This method of fraud exists also in wholesale.

Advantages of fraud detect

Any financial institution tends to have possibility to encounter fraud. According to the USA statistics, there are thousands of cases when organizations lost more than billions dollars annually because of the fraud. Banks and insurance companies are at the greatest risk of fraud and extortion. The thing is that fraud prevention services will save personal business from fraud in the Internet. Furthermore, avoid stealing money from account with the card fraud detection. Key role is played by historical data during the fraud website detection. Data could help to identify perpetrators of fraudulent activities. Online fraud detection demands the identification of evaluated subject for the timely detection of fraudulent activities. Being aware of everything what is happening, it is easier to be one step ahead. The earlier the fraud will be found, the better customer will protect himself.
